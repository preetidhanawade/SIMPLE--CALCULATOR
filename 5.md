A simple Python Calculator built using Python functions and conditional statements. This calculator can perform Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division, and Average calculations.

## Features

✅ Addition of two numbers  
✅ Subtraction of two numbers  
✅ Multiplication of two numbers  
✅ Division of two numbers  
✅ Average of two numbers
